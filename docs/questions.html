Panel discussion summary

The panel was deftly chaired by Rob Hoffman, Vice Chair of Young Engineers Australia Queensland, sitting in the centre along with the team of innovative panellists (from left to right):

Julie Mitchell – Deputy Director-General, Department of Transport and Main Roads 

Kellie Nuttall – Partner, Consulting Deloitte

Ryan Stuart – CEO & founder, Kapiche

James Gilmour – Director, Gilmour Space Technologies

As the audience descended to feed and water after the last question, the loud chatter marked the night a success, full of insight and provoked thought.

The following is a brief and fairly candid record of some of the interesting discussion topics and key take home messages and insights from the innovating with uncertainty event.
